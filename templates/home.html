About Me

I'm a graduate of the Pennsylvania State University with a BS in mechanical engineering. I'm also now a senior at the University of Pittsburgh pursuing a second BS in computer engineering. As a mechanical engineer I traveled to various power plants throughout the country, diagnosing and fixing vibrational problems in generators. I discovered a passion for coding when I bought an arduino and began working on home projects in my spare time.

Outside the classroom I'm the goaltender for the Pitt Women's Ice Hockey Club. At Penn State I was the Vice President and co-founder of the Penn State Women's Ice Hockey Club and was chosen to travel to Erzerum, Turkey and Trentino, Italy to play for Team USA in the World University Games.
